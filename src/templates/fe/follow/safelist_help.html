Follow Campaign Safelist

For the follow campaign to work effectively, we need unfollow some followers of your Twitter account.  It is possible you never want to unfollow certain followers.  In such case, you can put these followers into your safelists.

You can pick any list of your Twitter account to be a safelist.  If you are not familiar with Twitter lists yet, please visit this link on Twitter.com: http://help.twitter.com/forums/10711/entries/76460-twitter-lists

For best practice, a single Twitter list should not have more than 500 members.
