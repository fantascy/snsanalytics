  • Link to Full Articles

    Include links to a full atricle or any other page you choose. Use the "GridSlider" to feature important information in a way that visitors can easily find out more. Add links within your description, or use the built-in button.

  • Double Width Thumbnails

    Create variation in the layout and display of yor grid by assigning thumbnails to be different widths. Just give the item an additional class and the grid will automatically apply this wider thumbnail layout to any item you choose.

  • Hover to Preview, Click for More

    Hover over items to change slides and read the item description. While hovering the slide show will pause until you move your mouse to another item or move off the slide show area. Click an item to follow the "read more" link for that item.

  • Custom or Automatic Thumbnails

    Specify an image to use as the thumbnail in your grid, or allow the plugin to automatically generate one from your main slide. You have complete control over the display of your images.

    Include links to a full atricle or any other page you choose. Use the "GridSlider" to feature important information in a way that visitors can easily find out more. Add links within your description, or use the built-in button.

  • Overlays for Easy to Read Titles

    Sometimes text is hard to read over an image. Usually this is because the image color matches the text reducing contrast. Simply add the class "showBg" to any title and a translucent background will be displayed behind your text making it easy to read.
